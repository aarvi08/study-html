Deepshikha is a school for students from Pre-Nursery to Class 12. The school follows the syllabus prescribed by UP Board and the Central Board of Secondary Education.

Deepshikha believes that education is a continuous and creative process. Its aim is to develop the latent capacities of human nature & to coordinate their expression for the enrichment & progress of the society by equipping children with material, moral & spiritual knowledge.
